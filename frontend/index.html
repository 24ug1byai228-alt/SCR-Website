The Problem

Water Shortage

Rice cultivation traditionally requires 3000L of water for 1kg of paddy.

Unpredictable Climate

Indian agriculture is a "Gamble with Monsoons".

Wastage

Traditional furrow irrigation wastes massive amounts of water.

The Solution

  • Layer 1: Alluvial Soil for Roots.
  • Layer 2: Sand Filter.
  • Layer 3: Gravel & Pipe Recycling.

Impact: Saves 30% water (2.5L Liters/acre).
